HOW TO GET STARTED

Join the UAE’s largest network of volunteers, or post a request for volunteers to find the right fit for your needs.

  • Volunteer Now
  • Post a Volunteering Request
1

Sign up

Fill out your basic information on the portal (minimum age is 14).

2

Complete profile

Verify your email and mobile number and complete your profile

3

Find a cause

Browse through the latest volunteering causes and opportunities to find the one that matches your skill

4

Volunteer

Once you are shortlisted, it’s time to offer your skills, energy and passion where they are needed the most, whether virtually or in the field!

5

Receive a certificate

Share your volunteering experience with us and earn your volunteering certificate

1

Registration

Register your organization/company to access the largest national volunteering network today

2

Verification

Verify your email address and mobile number

3

Post an Opportunity

Post your volunteering needs and requests and connect with potential volunteers

4

Select volunteers

Shortlist the best matches for your needs and start benefiting from their help immediately

5

Report & Assessment

Once the project is complete, report back with the number of hours and your impact assessment
