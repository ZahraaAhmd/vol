The Virtual Majlis, brought to you by ADNOC Insights, in support of Volunteers.ae

From the labs of ADNOC Insights, the Virtual Majlis sees UAE leaders and global experts come together to ignite debate and address the most urgent challenges facing our world today.

The Virtual Majlis will support our community and society at large by bringing the sharpest minds to the table to deliver actionable insights to questions ranging from how to rebuild the global and domestic economy, to what the post-covid work place should look. The content it will deliver will be relevant, timely, and valuable to individuals, businesses and society.

-John Sfakianakis

Chief Economist & Head of research, Gulf Research and Visiting Scholar at Pembroke College, Cambriddge Univeristy

Dr. John Sfakianakis is an internationally renowned economist and global strategist, specializing on the macroeconomics of the Middle East and Emerging Markets, with a special focus on Gulf oil-producers, sovereigns and energy markets. John is a Senior Scholar at Pembroke College, University of Cambridge, an Associate Fellow of the Middle East and North Africa Program at Chatham House, London, and the Chief Economist and Head of Research of the Gulf Research Center.

-Jeremy White

Executive Editor, WIRED Magazibe

Jeremy White is currently the Executive Editor at WIRED Magazine. Jeremy curates the live product experiences at WIRED’s events, charged with bringing together exclusive displays showcasing the latest developments in automotive, technology, design and art. He is known for being able to articulate complex new technological developments in an exciting and relevant way that engages the audience and promotes new thinking. Jeremy appears regularly on the BBC and Sky News commenting on the latest tech trends.

-John Sfakianakis

Director of UCL Institute for Human Health and Performance Topics to be explored

Dr. Hugh Montgomery is Professor of Intensive Care Medicine at University College London (UCL), where he also directs the Centre for Human Health and Performance. He has published over 480 scientific research articles and is best known for his discover of ‘the first gene for human fitness.’ He has also worked in the field of Artificial Intelligence as applied to health, most recently for DeepMind and Google Health. Dr. Montgomery still spends 25% of his time working in a North London Intensive Care Unit. He is one of the first responders for Covid-19. He has become known for his ability to communicate the complexity of Covid-19 to a non-medical audience.”
